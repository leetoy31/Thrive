Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What should I bring to my first class?

Bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide all necessary equipment including mats, weights, and resistance bands.

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new members. This gives you full access to our facilities and classes to see if THRIVE is the right fit for you.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have free parking space available for all members. Located conveniently along CPG Avenue with easy access.

Are personal training sessions included?

Personal training sessions are included with our Premium and Elite plans. Basic members can add personal training sessions at discounted rates.

Do you have shower facilities?

Yes! We have clean, modern locker rooms with showers, lockers, and amenities. Towels are provided for all members.

How do I get to your gym?

We're located along CPG Avenue in Cogon District, easily accessible by tricycle, jeepney, or private vehicle. Landmark: near Island City Mall.
