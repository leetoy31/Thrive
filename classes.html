Our Classes

Discover the perfect workout for your fitness journey

Strength Training

Build muscle, increase strength, and transform your physique with our comprehensive strength training programs. Our expert trainers will guide you through proper form and technique to maximize results while preventing injury.

Duration: 60 minutes | Level: All Levels

Yoga Sessions

Find balance and flexibility through various yoga styles including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Power Yoga. Perfect for all skill levels, our yoga classes help improve flexibility, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being.

Duration: 45-90 minutes | Level: Beginner to Advanced

Cardio Blast

Burn calories and boost your cardiovascular health with high-energy cardio workouts. From HIIT to cycling, our cardio classes are designed to push your limits and deliver maximum results in minimum time.

Duration: 45 minutes | Level: Intermediate to Advanced

Calisthenics

Master bodyweight exercises and build functional strength with our calisthenics program. Learn advanced movements, improve coordination, and develop impressive control over your body without any equipment.

Duration: 60 minutes | Level: All Levels

Pilates

Strengthen your core and improve posture with our Pilates classes. Focus on controlled movements, breathing techniques, and building long, lean muscles while enhancing mind-body connection.

Duration: 50 minutes | Level: All Levels

Boxing & Kickboxing

Release stress and build power with our boxing and kickboxing classes. Learn proper technique, improve coordination, and get an intense full-body workout that burns calories and builds confidence.

Duration: 60 minutes | Level: All Levels
